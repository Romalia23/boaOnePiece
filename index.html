Boa Hancock

Boa Hancock, famously known as the "Pirate Empress", is a major character in the One Piece anime and manga series. She is the ruler of the all-female Amazon Lily, captain of the Kuja Pirates, and wields rare, highly coveted Conqueror's Haki.

Devil Fruit: Mero Mero no Mi

Petrification: Fires heart-shaped beams or produces arrows that turn anyone with impure or lustful thoughts toward her into stone.

Object Petrification: Can turn inanimate objects like arrows and cannonballs into stone.

Slashing Attacks: Can shatter her petrified opponents with a single physical strike.

Address: 1 Chome-23-10 Jinnan, Shibuya, Tokyo 150-0041, Japan
